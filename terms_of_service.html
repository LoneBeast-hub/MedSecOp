Professional Opinion, Not Diagnosis

The medical opinions provided on this platform are meant to **supplement** your existing diagnosis. They **do not** replace a physical examination or an in-person consultation with a licensed physician.

Consult Your Primary Healthcare Provider

The insights shared by physicians are meant to assist your conversations with your **family doctor or specialist**. Always consult your healthcare provider before making medical decisions.

No Patient-Physician Relationship Established

Using MedSecOp **does not establish** a formal patient-physician relationship. The second opinions offered are **advisory in nature** and should not be considered direct medical advice.

Accuracy and Completeness of Information

The accuracy of second opinions depends on the **medical records and images** you provide. MedSecOp and its physicians **are not responsible** for errors due to incomplete or inaccurate data.

Limitation of Liability

MedSecOp and its affiliated physicians **are not liable** for any actions taken based on the second opinions provided. Users agree to **indemnify and hold MedSecOp harmless** from any legal claims.

No Guarantee of Outcomes

Opinions on this platform are based on **medical expertise and available knowledge**. MedSecOp does not guarantee any **specific treatment results**.

Compliance with Laws and Regulations

MedSecOp follows strict **privacy and healthcare regulations** to ensure secure handling of patient data in accordance with **HIPAA and other laws**.
